6 Multani Mitti Face Packs For All Your Skin Concerns

Written by Ciyona Fernandes | Jun 21, 2022


From your Grandma to your favourite blogger, everyone has always been raving about Multani mitti and its goodness for the skin. Fuller's Earth, also known as Multani mitti, is a natural clay powder mask that leaves you with soft, smooth, and supple skin. So on a Sunday afternoon when your skin needs some extra TLC, whip some of these easy Multani mitti face packs for glowing skin. Mix and match the ingredients according to your skin’s needs and make the most out of this wondrous ingredient.



Multani Mitti For Dry Skin

To boost your skin with an extra dose of nourishment, you can use Multani mitti mixed with almond milk. So if your skin screams for moisture, this is the perfect DIY facepack for dry skin.


Ingredients:

1 tbsp ground almonds

1 tbsp raw milk

1 small cup Multani mitti


Method:

Make a smooth paste with Multani mitti, almond, and milk. Let it sit for a minute. Apply it on cleansed skin and once it’s dried off, scrub the pack off using a wet cloth.



Multani Mitti For Spotless Skin

Hate how dark spots are keeping you from the skin of your dreams? Well, a little tomato juice can help you get that glow sis. Tomato juice is an excellent exfoliant and adding it with Multani mitti will leave you with glowing skin, spotless skin. Mixing in a little turmeric and sandalwood powder will lighten the blemishes really well.


Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp tomato juice
  • 2 tbsp Multani mitti
  • 1 tsp sandalwood powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder


Method:

Make a thick paste by mixing all the ingredients. Apply it on your face for 10 minutes before rinsing it with warm water. Use this Multani mitti face pack for glowing skin regularly.



Multani Mitti For Glowing Skin

Nothing says healthy skin like a beautiful glow. Besides your everyday skincare routine, take an extra step to keep your glow going with DIY Multani mitti. Adding nourishing ingredients like honey and milk will keep your skin soft and supple whereas lemon and tomato juice will keep your skin from pesky spots.


Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp Multani mitti
  • 1 tsp tomato juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon raw milk


Method:

Make a paste of Multani mitti powder, tomato juice, honey, lemon juice, and some milk, apply it on your face, and leave it on for 20 minutes.



Multani Mitti For Sun Burns

If you spent more than a few hours basking in the sun, you probably have itchy and sunburned skin. Soothe your skin using the cooling goodness of Multani mitti, mint leaves, and yoghurt. This will not only heal your skin but also calm down the irritation.


Ingredients:

1 tbsp Multani mitti

1 tbsp mint leaves powder

1 tbsp yoghurt


Method:

Mix in Multani mitti, mint leaves powder and yoghurt. Apply the paste on the affected areas and leave it on for 20 minutes.



Multani Mitti For Even-Toned Skin

Spoiler alert - there’s no such thing as perfectly even-toned skin. But with simple homemade face mask recipes, you can get rid of hyperpigmentation, dark spots, and patches. All you need is a face pack made of nourishing ingredients like sandalwood powder, papaya pulp, Multani mitti, and raw milk. Because Multani mitti has a very mild bleaching effect, it aids in reducing pigmentation and leaves you with healthy skin.


Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Multani mitti
  • 1 tbsp sandalwood powder
  • 1 teaspoon raw milk


Method:

Mix sandalwood powder with Multani mitti and a few drops of milk. Apply over your face for 30 minutes and keep it on for some time. Then, rinse with water.



Multani Mitti For Acne Scars

If you have popped that pimple (even if you shouldn’t) you must be having a bigger problem than the pimple- the scars. Excessive oil secretion on your skin can create a lot of acne and that’s where Multani mitti and rose water come in. Both these ingredients are great at zapping out the excess oil from your skin making it clearer and smoother.


Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Multani mitti
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp rose water


Method:

Blend all the ingredients to form a smooth paste. Apply this face pack on your freshly washed skin and let it dry for about half an hour. Rinse it off with lukewarm water.
